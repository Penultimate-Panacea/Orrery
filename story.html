Story Phase

Going around the table, each Wizard chooses one of the following:
  • Take back a Week of Time. The Wizard briefly (in one to two sentences) narrates what he did during that week, and makes the necessary adjustments
  • Take back a Week of Time and a Star The Wizard plays out a Scene with any Characters who would be present (played by the Celestial Audiance). Regardless of what happens during this Scene, the Wizard receives the benefits of Spending Time in that way.
  • Take back a Star. The Wizard sets the Scene with the other Character played by another character, or the Celestial Audience as a whole.
  • Move a Week of Time he hasn't yet taken back. A Wizard can only choose this once a month.
  • If the Wizard's only Token left is the one on the Wizardmoot, he instead passes to the next Wizard.
If a Wizard is no longer able to Spend Time in the fashion he has arranged, the week is wasted. For instance, the Necromancer needs his Pact-Fragment to rebuff the Foes of Death. If he no longer has his Pact-Fragment, he cannot rebuff these Foes.
Leave the Time Spent on the Meeting Phase. Continue in this Fasion unil the only Weeks of Time are those on the Meeting Phase.

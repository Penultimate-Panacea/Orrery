Meeting Phase

All Wizards who Spent Time on the Meeting Phase attend the Wizardmoot. It's run by the Archmagus (or, if he isn't able to attend, it is run by the next-eldest Wizard). The Wizardmoot is a Scene, during which Wizards are encouraged to discuss the following topics in-character:
  • How you and your Domain fared during the month.
  • What the next month holds for you.
  • Whether you will need any help in future months.
As each Wizard leaves the Wizardmoot, he takes his Token back frorm the Meeting Phase.

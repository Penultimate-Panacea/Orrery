Planning Phase

Each Wizard places four Weeks of Time. They can Spend Time in the following areas:
  • Upon the Meeting Phase, to attend the Wizardmoot.
  • Upon the Orrery, to move any planet forward or backwards an amount equal to its Arc. Only if the Orrery is accessible
  • Upon any of his Companions, to gain +1 in the Compainion's associated Element for the duration of the month.
  • Upon the Lore of his Isle or Sanctum, in order to change or add to it
  • Upon the Grimoire, to cast a spell Patiently
  • Upon his Domain, to engage with the rules of his Domain
Wizards cannot coordinate with each other while Spending Time. Wizards can Spend Time within another Wizard's Domain, but only on tasks they can perform with only their Pact-Fragment and Humble Magic.
Once all Wizards have decided how to Spend their Time, they each place a Star. Stars can be placed:
  • With one of your own Weeks of Time, to have a Scene during that Week as part of your action there
  • With another Wizard, to have a Scene with them
  • With one of the Wizard's Companions, to have a scene with them (during which the Wizard can ask for a favor)
  • With any other Character, to appear before them and have a Scene with them
  • You cannot place a Star on the Meeting Phase

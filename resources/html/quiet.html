Quiet Phase

At the end of the Month, everything is tidied up.
  • Certain Wizards' Domains have events which occur at the end of the month, during this Phase
  • If any Wizards died during the Month, a Funeral is held for them.
  • Certain Ages have End of Season or End of Year Rituals which are held during this Phase.
  • Take a break to drink water, eat snacks, and rest.

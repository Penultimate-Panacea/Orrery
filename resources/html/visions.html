Visions Phase

Each Wizard consults the "Watching the Stars" section of their Codex, and makes changes to their domain corresponding with the position of the Celestial Bodies in the sky.
Once all changes have been made to each Wizard's Domain, they provide any Impact produced during this phase to other Wizard's Domains.
While it's possible for the Celestial Bodies to move during later Phases of the month, the effects of Watching the Stars occurs once each month, during the Visions Phase.
